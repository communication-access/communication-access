Participant response notebook

A collaborative document to help individuals with complex communication access needs (CCAN), like AAC users, actively participate in online meetings. It allows them to input their responses before, during, or after a meeting, to address communication barriers they may face during online meetings and larger group discussions. Meeting organizers can customize and share this template in advance for attendees to prepare and contribute.
